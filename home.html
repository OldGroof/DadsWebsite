Company Background

Finance for International Trade Ltd. is an independent advisor and arranger of trade and export finance solutions for companies trading internationally.

Working with a broad network of banks, insurance underwriters, specialist finance funds, export credit agencies and other non-bank financial institutions, we arrange and structure trade and export finance facilities to support international trade worldwide. The company focusses on providing bespoke solutions rather than off the shelf products and is able to arrange facilities that are not available from conventional banking/financing sources.

We have an extensive client base of exporting companies engaged in manufacturing, engineering, construction, procurement, project management and commodity trading.

The company is seen by our clients as a “go-to” solutions provider when difficult payment terms, trade with higher risk emerging markets or long term export credits are involved and present obstacles to business going ahead. Our extensive network of banks, insurers and specialist non-bank financial institutions worldwide enables us to arrange the solution to fit the specific financing or payment protection need. Clients often come to us for assistance when their house banks and insurers have been unable to help them.

The company is privately owned and registered in England. We are based south of London, working mostly with UK and European exporters, but also have clients in the US, the Middle East and the Far East.

Facilities Arranged

Finance for exports to higher risk countries and emerging markets

Financing and payment collection services for sales to challenging territories in the Middle East, Africa, Eastern Europe, Asia, the Far East and South America

Export Credits and Project Finance

Supported by Export Credit Agency or private market insurance/guarantees.

Letter of Credit Finance

Confirmation, discounting, documentation management and training to ensure security and promptness of payment and avoid discrepancies. Confirmation and discounting can be arranged for markets or issuing banks declined by conventional house banks

Non-recourse Finance and discounting of trade receivables

Including Bills of Exchange, Promissory Notes, Lease receivables, Vendor Payment Plan payments and open account invoices.

Payment Guarantees and Credit Insurance

Single risk or whole turnover credit insurance, payment guarantees, standby L/C’s and silent confirmation

Payment Collection

In respect of current and overdue payments from emerging markets that house banks are unable to accept.

Bonding

Issue of performance bonds and advance payment guarantees into emerging markets.

Bespoke Finance

For financing trade with countries where conventional banking/insurance solutions are unavailable.
